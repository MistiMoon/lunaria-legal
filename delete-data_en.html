Deleting Lunaria app data

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Lunaria does not create user accounts

The app does not require registration, a login, password, or email address. Profile data such as name, birth date, and birth time is stored locally on the device. When a personalized reading is generated, some data is temporarily sent to Firebase and Gemini services. The latest reading and data needed for feature limits may be stored in Firebase under a technical device identifier.

Delete data from your phone

Open your profile in the app and choose the logout/profile deletion option. You can also clear the app data in Android settings or uninstall Lunaria.

Request deletion of cloud data

To request deletion of data associated with the technical device identifier, contact us below with “Lunaria data deletion” in the subject. We may ask for additional information needed to locate the data. Requests will be handled within 30 days.
