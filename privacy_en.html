Privacy Policy for the Lunaria app

Last updated: 30 June 2026

1. General information

This Privacy Policy sets out the rules for processing user data in the Lunaria mobile application ("App").

The data controller is:
MistiMoon
Contact: mistimoon.app@gmail.com

The App was created to provide content related to Tarot, horoscopes, angel cards, personal development and spiritual reflection.

2. What data may be processed

The App does not require users to create an account, register, or provide an email address or password.

To personalize some features, the user may voluntarily provide:

  • name;
  • date of birth;
  • time of birth (optional);
  • selected app language;
  • notification settings;
  • history of using selected features.

Most of this data is stored locally on the user’s device.

3. Technical data

When using the App, some Google services may process technical data necessary for the proper operation of the services, such as:

  • anonymous advertising identifiers;
  • diagnostic information;
  • service performance data;
  • information about app errors.

4. Firebase services

The App uses Firebase services provided by Google LLC.

In particular, the following services are used:

  • Firestore Database — storing and retrieving app data.
  • Firebase Functions — secure communication with external services, including the Gemini API.
  • Firebase App Check — protecting the app infrastructure against abuse.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — handling push notifications if this feature is made available to users.

More information:
https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5. Integration with Gemini AI

Some content available in the App is generated using Gemini artificial intelligence models provided by Google.

The information sent is used only to generate a response for the user. Data is not used for user profiling or for selling personal data.

Content generated by artificial intelligence is:

  • entertainment-oriented;
  • reflective;
  • supportive of personal development.

AI-generated content does not constitute:

  • medical advice;
  • psychological advice;
  • legal advice;
  • financial advice.

6. Ads (Google AdMob)

The App may display personalized or non-personalized ads in accordance with user settings and Google requirements.

Ads help finance the development and maintenance of the project. Google AdMob may process data in accordance with its own privacy policy.

More information:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. In-app purchases and subscriptions

The App may offer paid features, subscriptions and digital purchases.

Payments are handled through Google Play Billing.

Purchases are assigned to the user’s Google account used in the Google Play Store. The App administrator does not have access to users’ payment card details, billing data or financial information.

8. Notifications

The App may send notifications related to:

  • horoscopes;
  • cards of the day;
  • angel cards;
  • messages;
  • reminders.

The user may disable notifications at any time in the device settings.

9. User rights

Because the App does not require users to create an account, most data is stored locally on the device.

The user may:

  • view their data;
  • modify their data;
  • delete app data;
  • uninstall the App.

10. Contact

Any questions about this Privacy Policy may be sent to:
mistimoon.app@gmail.com

11. Changes to the Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the App develops, as technology changes, or as applicable law changes.

The current version of the document will always be available:

  • in the App;
  • on the Lunaria project website.
